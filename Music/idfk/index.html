          about

          From the Archives of the Wasteland Wailers is the "unofficial official OST" of the Ashes of Equestria, an RPG based on KKat's book Fallout: Equestria, being developed by the Overmare Studios since 2013. The album contains 19 tracks made between 2013 and 2017, revised at the end of 2020.

          about

          The Wasteland Wailers Antarctica

          We are a Transatlantic group of songwriters and music producers who were making music over the Internet before it was cool.

          Not really from Antarctica. It's just that we aren't from a single place, not even from the same country :-)

          Proudly affiliated with the Overmare Studios and the entire Brony community. Most of our stuff is related to KKat's Fallout: Equestria fanfic.           ... more
